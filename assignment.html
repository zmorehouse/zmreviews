1.2 Your Me and My Passion

Link To Me and My Passion

This website looks to be a place to showcase my movie reviews.

I've looked to design this website in an editorial style, being inspired by the likes of Pitchfork and Vogue. As a result, I opted for a greyscale color pallette, being mixed alongside some colorful imagery and a pairing of both Serif and Sans-Serif fonts. Sharp edges and justified text help reinforce this.

Please note that the reusable sections of the site (header and footer) have been built through /scripts.js. I was conflicted whether to do this, as the response on my forum post (below) indicated I was allowed to, whereas David's post indicated I couldn't. I left this in, however didn't implement any other Javascript (and will wait for p2 & p3 to continue)

Please also view the website in a 1920 * 1080 desktop ratio. I have yet to add media commands (which will be added in p3)
